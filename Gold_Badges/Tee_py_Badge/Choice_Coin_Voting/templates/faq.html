  • Added new Database models. Positions, Candidate, Config
  • Adding automatic generation of candidate algorand address and asset-optin for candidates accounts.
  • Setting Voting Configs like escrow address.
  • Using ChartJS to visualize voting results.
  • Allows creation of multiple candidates and positions.
  • Adding utility functions to generate algorand address, opt-in asset into candidate's account, send-alogrand, validating escrow address and mnemonic, check_algorand balance.
